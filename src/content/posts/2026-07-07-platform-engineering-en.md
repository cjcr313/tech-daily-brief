DevOps promised 'You build it, you run it'. But the reality is that asking a frontend developer to be an expert in React, Kubernetes, Terraform, Helm, and Datadog generated unsustainable cognitive fatigue.

### Platform Engineering is Born
Instead of demanding everyone know everything, Platform teams build internal products. They provide developer portals (like Backstage) where deploying a database or a new microservice takes a single click, not 500 lines of YAML.

### Golden Paths
The key to success are *Golden Paths*: standardized, officially supported routes that do the right thing by default. If you stray from the path, you are free to do so, but you lose Platform team support.
